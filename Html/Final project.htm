About Cyber Swachata

Cyber Swachata is a pioneering initiative dedicated to promoting cyber hygiene and safety across India. Our mission is to create a secure digital environment for all citizens by providing resources, education, and support in combating cyber crimes.

We offer a user-friendly platform for reporting cyber incidents, access to expert advice, and comprehensive learning materials to empower individuals with the knowledge to navigate the digital world safely.
